110 GYMS IN INDIA

Anytime Fitness is celebrating its achievement of the breakthrough 100 Clubs in India. This Brand has been operating in India for the last eight years and has shown a rampant growth in terms of establishing its presence across India. Owing to the splendid response from its Club members and its unique culture in fitness industry, Anytime Fitness has continued to add newer Clubs to their portfolio ultimately enhancing its overall momentum.
